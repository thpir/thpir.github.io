About Me

My name is Thpir and I'm a self-thought Android™ developer. I currently work as a "project-engineer" in an R&D team of a company active in the automotive industry. It is my job to turn a "concept" into a "product". Prior to my work as a Project Engineer, I've been working in the wind industry as a service technician for wind turbines.


I've always had a fascination for software development, and am (ever since the Blackberry came to life) a huge enthousiast for Smartphones. As a boy-scout I found the pocket knife a fascinating tool, it had so many functinonalities while still fitting in your pocket. Well... I think smartphones are modern man's pocket knife. When it comes to developing applications to make one's live easier, the possibilities are endless. This is what fascinates me so much.


I didn't study software-engineering in college though. Software-development has always been like a "black-box" for me. It is only in 2021 that I started to explore the fascinating world of software engineering. In August 2021, I found a tutorial online to create your first "hello world!" app in Android Studio. In January 2022, I released my fist "basic" application on my Github page. More information can be found here . Since then I've been spending as much time as I could spare (while raising three kids) to improve my skills as a developer. I hope to call myself a professional android developer one day!
